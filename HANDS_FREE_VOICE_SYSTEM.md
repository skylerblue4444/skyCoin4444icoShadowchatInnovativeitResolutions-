## 3. Voice Command Execution

### 3.1 Confirmation Process

For high-value trades, users can enable voice confirmation:

1. **User says**: "Buy 1000 SKYCOIN4444"
2. **System confirms**: "Did you say buy 1000 SKYCOIN4444 at market price? Say yes to confirm or no to cancel."
3. **User says**: "Yes"
4. **System executes**: "Trade executed. You bought 1000 SKYCOIN4444 at 10.52. Your total cost is $10,520."

### 3.2 Auto-Execution Threshold

Users can set a confidence threshold for automatic execution without confirmation:

- **95-100%**: Auto-execute immediately
- **85-94%**: Require voice confirmation
- **75-84%**: Display confirmation prompt (voice or text)
- **Below 75%**: Always require confirmation

### 3.3 Voice Command History

All voice commands are logged and can be reviewed:

**Command**: "Show my voice command history"

**Response**: "Here are your recent voice commands:
1. Buy 100 SKYCOIN4444 - Executed at 10.52
2. Show portfolio - Displayed
3. Enable Hunhidge Mode - Activated
4. Ask Hope for advice - Received recommendation"
